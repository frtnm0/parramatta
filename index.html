Admin Reference Guide

Setting Up a New Staff Member in Microsoft 365 Admin Center

Est. Time: 10 mins 5 Main Steps Verified Process
💡 Tip for Advanced Admins: You can click the "Show Screenshots" switch in the navigation bar at any time to hide the images and view a streamlined, text-only guide.
Step 1

Create User in M365 Admin Center

Click to collapse
1.1

Open your browser, navigate to the Microsoft 365 Admin Center and log in using your administrator credentials.

  • In the left-hand navigation pane, select Users → Active users.
  • Click the Add a user button in the top toolbar.
Click to Zoom
1.2

In the Set up the basics flyout pane, fill in the following details:

  • First name & Last name of the employee.
  • Display name (automatically generated, verify format).
  • Username (create using corporate format: first.last or initial.last).

Verify that the correct corporate domain is selected in the dropdown (e.g. @yourcompany.com).

Under password settings, ensure the following checkboxes are checked:

  • Automatically create a password (ensures high entropy).
  • Require this user to change password when they first sign in (crucial for zero-trust security).

Click Next at the bottom of the pane.

Click to Zoom
1.3

In the Assign product licenses pane:

  1. Select the correct Location / country where the employee resides.
  2. Tick the checkbox for Microsoft 365 Business Standard.
  3. Click Next.
Click to Zoom
1.4

In the Optional settings pane, expand the Profile info section to add company metadata:

  • Job Title (matching their contract).
  • Department (e.g. Sales, Finance, IT).
  • Contact Number (if they have a corporate mobile assigned).

Click Next.

Click to Zoom
1.5

In the Review and finish pane, carefully double-check all details, spelling, and usernames. Once reviewed, click Finish adding.

Click to Zoom
1.6

Verify that the account was successfully created. Click Close.

⚠️ CRITICAL: Do not close the window without copying down the temporary password! You will need this for the manager confirmation email in Section 5.
Click to Zoom
Step 2

Assign a Business Standard License

Click to collapse
2.1

If you bypassed licensing during account setup, or need to verify/modify the licenses for an existing profile:

  1. Search for the user in the Active users search bar.
  2. Click directly on their display name to expand the side-flyout detailed view.
  3. Navigate to the Licenses and apps tab
  4. Select Microsoft 365 Business Standard
  5. click Save changes.
Click to Zoom
Step 3

Enroll the User in MFA

Click to collapse

⚠️ NOTE: This guide is for Per-user MFA setup


Microsoft recommends managing MFA exclusively through Conditional Access and the unified Authentication Methods policy. This approach automatically and dynamically applies to all existing users and new hires.


Per-User MFA is a legacy feature and must be set to "Disabled" to prevent login conflicts and policy bypasses.


Only follow this guide if no Conditional Access has been set.

3.1

To enforce immediate Multi-Factor Authentication enrollment for the user account:

From the Users → Active users menu in Microsoft 365 Admin Center, search and select the newly created user.

Click to Zoom
3.2

Under the account pane, locate Manage multifactor authentication

This may open in a separate Microsoft MFA page (Entra Admin Center).

Click to Zoom
3.3
  1. In the Microsoft MFA page that opened, search for the new user.
  2. Tick the checkbox beside the new user.
  3. Click Enable MFA
Click to Zoom
3.4

Confirm Enable multi-factor auth by clicking Enable

Click to Zoom
3.5

Verify the status changes to Enabled

Click to Zoom
Step 4

Add User to "All Staff" Distribution List Group

Click to collapse
4.1

To ensure the new hire receives global internal corporate announcements:

In the M365 Admin Center left-hand side navigation pane, expand Teams & groups and click on Active teams & groups.

Navigate to the Distribution list tab, find the All Staff group list entry, and click directly on it to slide open its management pane.

Click to Zoom
4.2

In the All Staff details pane:

  1. Navigate to the Members tab at the top.
  2. Click on the View all and manage members link.
Click to Zoom
4.3

Click + Add members in the toolbar.

Click to Zoom
4.4
  1. Search for the newly created staff member in the search box
  2. Select the checkbox next to their name
  3. click Add.
Click to Zoom
4.5

Confirm the user now appears in the member list.

Click to Zoom
Step 5

Send Onboarding Confirmation Email to the Manager

Click to collapse
5.1

Open Outlook or your corporate mail client. Draft a new email to the manager of the newly hired staff member.

Use the professional pre-written email template below. Highlight, copy, and replace all brackets [like this] with the actual employee details.

SUBJECT: New Account Created - John Smith

Email Body Template


Dear [Manager Name],

The Microsoft 365 user account has been successfully provisioned and configured for our new staff member. Below are their login credentials and setup guidelines.

  1. Account Details
    • Full Name: [New Hire Name]
    • Job Title: [Job Title]
    • Department: [Department Name]
    • Email: [new.hire@company.com]
    • Temporary Password: [Provide securely or insert here]

  2. First-Time Login & MFA Setup Guide
    Please share these exact steps with the new hire on their first day:


    1. Download the App: Install the Microsoft Authenticator app on your mobile phone via the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
    2. Go to Portal: On your computer, open a web browser and go to microsoftonline.com.
    3. Sign In: Enter your company email and temporary password.
    4. Update Password: Create a new, secure password when prompted.
    5. Set Up MFA: A message saying "More information required" will appear. Click Next.
    6. Scan the QR Code: Choose "Mobile app" as your verification method, select "Receive notifications," and click Set up. Use your phone's Authenticator app to scan the QR code shown on your computer screen.
    7. Verify: Follow the final on-screen prompt to test the notification and complete your setup.Best regards,

Best regards,
